Here is a sample application for sick leave:

[Your Name]
[Your Address]
[City, State, Pincode]
[Date]

[Recipient's Name]
[Recipient's Designation]
[School/College/Company Name]
[School/College/Company Address]
[City, State, Pincode]

Dear [Recipient's Name],

I am writing to request for a sick leave on [Date(s) of leave] as I am currently unwell and unable to attend [school/college/work].

I am suffering from [mention the illness or medical condition, e.g. fever, flu, etc.]. My doctor has advised me to take complete rest and not to engage in any strenuous activities for the next [number] days.

I am expecting to recover soon and will make sure to catch up on any missed work as soon as I am feeling better. I apologize for any inconvenience this may cause and will make sure to complete all my tasks as soon as possible.

I would be grateful if you could approve my request for sick leave. If there is any additional information required, please let me know.

Thank you for your understanding and support.

Sincerely,

[Your Signature]
[Your Name]

Please note that you should customize this letter as per your needs and the requirements of your school/college/company. Also, be sure to include any necessary documentation, such as a doctor's note, to support your request.
